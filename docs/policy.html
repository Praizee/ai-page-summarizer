Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Short version: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Your API key never leaves your browser except to call the AI provider you chose.

What data this extension handles

AI Page Summarizer handles the following data, all of which stays on your device:

  • API key — stored in chrome.storage.local, accessible only to this extension. Never sent to any server we operate.
  • Page content — text extracted from the current tab is sent directly to the AI provider you selected (Groq, Google Gemini, or OpenAI). It is not routed through any intermediate server.
  • Summary cache — generated summaries are stored in chrome.storage.local to avoid duplicate API calls. Cached data stays on your device and is never transmitted.

What we do NOT collect

  • No personal information (name, email, account details)
  • No browsing history
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No cookies
  • No data sent to any server operated by this extension

Third-party AI providers

When you summarize a page, its text content is sent to the AI provider you configured in Settings. Each provider has its own privacy policy:

  • Groq Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

You are responsible for reviewing the terms of your chosen provider. This extension does not endorse or control any third-party service.

Permissions

This extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — to save your API key and summary cache locally
  • activeTab — to read the content of the current tab when you click Summarize
  • scripting — to inject the content extraction script into the active tab on demand

No other permissions are requested. The extension does not access tabs you haven't explicitly triggered it on.

Data retention

All data (API key and cached summaries) is stored locally in your browser and can be removed at any time by clicking Remove Key in Settings or by uninstalling the extension. Summaries are automatically evicted after 24 hours and the cache is capped at 50 entries.

Children's privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
